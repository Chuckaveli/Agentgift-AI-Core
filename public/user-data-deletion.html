Request to Delete Your Data

If you have signed up or logged in to AgentGift using Facebook, Google, or any third-party provider and wish to delete your data from our system, you can submit a deletion request.

How to Delete Your Data

You have two options:

  • Email us: Send a request to support@agentgift.ai with the subject line Delete My Account.
  • Automatic: If you're logged in, go to Settings → Delete Account in your dashboard (if available).

We will process your request within 7 days and delete all user data associated with your account, including login credentials, profile information, and activity.
