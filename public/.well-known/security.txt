# Security Disclosure Policy

## Reporting Security Issues

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in AgentGift.ai, 
we encourage you to let us know right away.

Please email security@agentgift.ai with:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Your contact information

## Response Timeline

- Acknowledgment: Within 24 hours
- Initial assessment: Within 48 hours
- Status update: Within 7 days
- Fix deployed: Depends on severity (1-30 days)

## Bug Bounty Program

We offer rewards for valid security vulnerabilities:
- Critical: $500-$2000
- High: $250-$500
- Medium: $100-$250
- Low: $50-$100

## Out of Scope

- Social engineering
- Physical attacks
- DoS/DDoS attacks
- Spam
- Issues in third-party services

Thank you for helping keep AgentGift.ai safe!
